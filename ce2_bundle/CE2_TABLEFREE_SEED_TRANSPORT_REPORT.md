## What was proven
Let the CE2 closed-form coefficient tables in `scripts/ce2_global_cocycle.py` be
- `C0[t][d](s,w)` and `E[t][d](s,w)` for `t∈{1,2}` and directions `d∈F3^2\{0}`.
Fix seed direction `d0=(1,0)`. For each `d`, pick any `A_d∈SL(2,3)` with `A_d d0=d` and set `B=A_d^{-1}`.
Define pullback on invariants by:
1) recover `u_c` from `(s,w,d)` via the invertible `d`-frame map,
2) send `u_c' = B u_c`, then recompute `(s',w')` in the seed frame.

Then the committed tables satisfy an exact seed-transport law:

`C0[t][d](s,w) = C0[t][d0](s',w') + ΔC0_B(w)`

`E[t][d](s,w)  = E[t][d0](s',w')  + ΔE_{B,t}(s,w)`

with Δ-polynomials of degree ≤2 in each variable. **ΔC0 depends only on B and only on w**, and **ΔE depends on B and t**.
