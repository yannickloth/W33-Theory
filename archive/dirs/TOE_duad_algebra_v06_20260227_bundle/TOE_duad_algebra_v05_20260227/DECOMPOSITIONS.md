# Decompositions of natural permutation modules (computed from class characters)

All decompositions are over C for G = PSp(4,3) (order 25920), using class inner-products.

## 120 duads (perfect matchings on lines)
Multiplicity-free:
- 120 = 1 ⊕ 20 ⊕ 15 ⊕ 24 ⊕ 60

## 40 points (projective points of F3^4)
Not multiplicity-free against the duad-spherical set; splits as:
- 40 = 1 ⊕ 24 ⊕ 15'

where 15' is a **second** (non-spherical) 15-dimensional irrep (orthogonal to the spherical 15).

## 40 lines (totally isotropic 2-spaces)
- 40 = 1 ⊕ 15 ⊕ 24

## 240 edges (collinear pairs / W33 edges)
- 240 = (1 ⊕ 20 ⊕ 15 ⊕ 2·24 ⊕ 60) ⊕ 15' ⊕ 81

Here 81 is an irreducible degree-81 character, and 15' is the same one from the 40-point action.
