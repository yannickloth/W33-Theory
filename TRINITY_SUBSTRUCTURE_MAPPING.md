- **Leech (24):**
	- The 24 dimensions of the Leech lattice are constructed from the binary Golay code; a ternary analog may yield a new 24D lattice with M₁₂ symmetry.

- **Albert (27):**
	- The 27 lines on a cubic surface and the 27D exceptional Jordan algebra appear as modules in the decomposition of the Leech lattice and Monster representations.
