Let K be the group generated by the 5 permutations on the 54-pocket orbit. We compute:

- |K| = 162
- K is transitive on the 27 twin-pairs (stabilizer size 6)

### C) The derived subgroup is a **Heisenberg group of order 27**

On the 27-object, the commutator subgroup [K,K] has:

- order 27
- center of order 3
- derived subgroup of order 3

It acts **regularly** on the 27 objects.

We choose explicit generators `a,b` with central commutator `c=[a,b]`.

> (x,y,z)*(x',y',z') = (x+x', y+y', z+z' - y*x') mod 3
